eCommerce Sales Analysis — MySQL

This project provided end-to-end e-commerce insights: revenue trends, product/category profitability, CLV & retention, seasonality, and geography.

Tables: customers, products, orders, order_items.

Analyses:

  • Monthly Revenue Trend (MoM growth & %)
  • Top Products by Revenue (units, profit, margin)
  • Customer Lifetime Value (segments: VIP/Loyal/Regular/New)
  • Category Performance (revenue, profit, margin, rank)
  • Customer Retention (avg days between first 5 orders)
  • Seasonal Sales Pattern (by month)
  • Geographic Sales (state/country, rev/customer)

HR Analytics & Employee Management — MySQL

This project analyzed turnover, pay, performance, training, managers, tenure, hiring, and high performers.

Tables: departments, employees, performance_reviews, training_programs, employee_training.

Analyses:

  • Turnover: rate & risk by department
  • Salary: min/max/avg/stdev/median by dept & role
  • Performance: latest scores, goals met, promo-ready
  • Training Effectiveness: participants, pass rate, ROI
  • Manager Effectiveness: span of control & team outcomes
  • Tenure: service brackets with KPIs
  • Hiring Trends: hires by month/year
  • High Performers: star/high/performer flags
